Beauty and the Beast





Director Bill Condon
Genre Musical, Romance, Fantasy, Kids & Family
Runtime 2h 9m
Original Language English








Description

Belle (Emma Watson), a bright, beautiful and independent young woman, is taken prisoner
by a beast (Dan Stevens) in its castle. Despite her fears, she befriends the castle's enchanted
staff and learns to look beyond the beast's hideous exterior, allowing her to recognize the
kind heart and soul of the true prince that hides on the inside.
